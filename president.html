What we know

  1. The Board of Trustees hasn't said much about the terms of Ferguson's resignation.
  2. Terry King won't be Ball State's next president.
  3. If finding Ferguson was any indication, the presidential search will cost about $150,000.
  4. The search committee is looking for "the school's goals," "transparency" and "diversity" in the new president.
  5. The presidential search is moving along, but is "closed" so little is known about the progress or applicants.
  6. The process is taking longer than most presidential searches.
  7. The search committee has presented the Board of Trustees with their final choices.

What we don't know

  1. When the next president will be announced.
  2. Who the next president will be.
  3. Exactly why Paul Ferguson left, though a December article by The Muncie Star-Press shed some light onto tensions between trustees and the former president.
